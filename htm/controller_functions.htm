Hand Controller Functions

Hand Controller Functions vary depending on the vehicle configuration. Functions include:

  • Vehicle Control
    • Forward / Reverse Control
    • Lateral Control
    • Yaw Control
    • Depth Control
    • Pitch Up
    • Pitch Down

  • Lights Control
    • Lights On
    • Lights Off
    • Lights Intensity

  • Camera Control
    • Camera Select
    • Camera Tilt Up
    • Camera Tilt Down
    • Camera Focus In
    • Camera Focus Out
    • Camera Zoom In
    • Camera Zoom Out
    • Snapshot
    • record,Video Record

  • Software Control
    • Cycle Display Mode

  • Manipulator Control
    • Manipulator Open
    • Manipulator Close
    • Manipulator Rotate

Some of the above functions are optional or may not apply to a specific vehicle configuration.
