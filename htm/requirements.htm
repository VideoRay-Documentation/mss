The VideoRay MSS Defender requires an external power source capable of producing 100 - 240 V DC; 50, 60 Hz output with a minimum steady-state capacity of 3,000 Watts.

The Defender ROV weighs approximately 34 kg (75 pounds) in its case. The ability to lift at least that much weight is required to transport and deploy the ROV.

Using trained operators and technicians is highly recommended.
