Sonar

The multibeam sonar provides the operator with a forward-looking image of the ROV's surrounding environment even when visibility is limited. The vehicle auto pitch can be used to aim the sonar up or down to improve the sonar imaging quality whether looking for targets on the bottom or the underside of a ship's hull.
