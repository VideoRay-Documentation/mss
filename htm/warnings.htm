The accessory port must be sealed with a terminated accessory connector or the accessory port terminator dummy plug. Failure to seal the accessory port may lead to loss of control of the ROV or damage to the components.

Do not block the User Control Console fans. Blocking the fans can lead to overheating and component failure.

Make sure to loosen the display monitor tilt arm before closing the User Control Console lid, and be careful when closing the lid to avoid damaging the computer or monitor or pinching any cables.

Always secure the tether connectors using the locking sleeves and strain relief system to avoid separation and loss of the ROV.

The tether connectors should be kept clean to avoid abrasion and corrosion on the electrical contacts and damage to the rubber insulation. Tether connectors should not be lubricated with petroleum products or grease. Petroleum will degrade the rubber and grease will attract dirt and lead to abrasion and corrosion. VideoRay recommends lubricating the tether connectors with pure silicone spray.

These values are breaking strength. The tether should not be subjected to a working strength greater than one half of the breaking strength. The ROV and tether are equipped with a strain relief cable and connectors, which are rated at 136 kilograms (300 pounds). The strain relief cable should be used to avoid separation of the tether connectors and loss of the ROV.

Use care when cleaning the pressure sensor to avoid damaging the sensor. Do not insert anything into the pressure sensor cavity, and do not apply high pressure spray to the sensor.

VideoRay ROVs and their components can be damaged by improper care and handling. The following sections provide guidelines and procedures for working with these components.

Failure to inspect and replace thruster cartridge seals regularly is one of the most common reasons for systems to be returned to VideoRay for repair. Water ingress through the thruster cartridge seals typically leads to major internal component damage, which is expensive to repair. View example cartridge seals.

Do not use cleaners that contain alcohol or other solvents. Solvents can make the domes brittle.

The display port cable has a latching connector. Make sure to press the latch button before trying to remove the connector.

Fuse GPS should only be used when Station Keeping and Waypoint Following is Off. If either is on when Fuse GPS is selected, the ROV may make a rapid and unexpected excursion.

Failure to properly maintain the ROV by thoroughly cleaning it after use may dramatically reduce its service life.

Connecting or disconnecting cables while the system is powered on is not recommended.
