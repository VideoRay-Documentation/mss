1. Connect the male ROV whip to the female tether and secure the connection with the locking collar.


2. Fold the ROV whip back on itself and tuck the connection up inside the cavity under the float block on the starboard side.


3. Insert the strain relief loop into the hole in the frame and secure it with the retaining screw.


4. Make sure the connection is secure.

For extra security, the tether can be secured to the frame using a cable tie.
