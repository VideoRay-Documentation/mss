Thruster Module

MSS vehicles use a modular thruster configuration that allows vehicle designs to be optimized for water conditions and payload delivery requirements. Modular thrusters are also easy to replace in the field.

Thruster Propeller

The propellers are designed to be used on counter-rotating thruster arrangements to eliminate torque roll or yaw. Propellers are identified by their pitch orientation using left and right based on their use on VideoRay Pro 4 ROVs.

  • Left-handed propellers have blades that appear to curve clockwise when viewed from the rear.
  • Right-handed propellers have blades that appear to curve counterclockwise when viewed from the rear.
