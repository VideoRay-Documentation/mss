GPS

GPS (Global Positioning System) is used to locate the latitude and longitude of the ROV and the deployment location (vessel or dock).

Topside GPS is used to position the vessel or area where the pilot is working.

  • Lowrance - High Precision GPS
  • USB 5 Hz - Portable GPS

Only one topside GPS is needed.

VideoRay ROV GPS is located on the vehicle to determine the position of the ROV while above the water.
